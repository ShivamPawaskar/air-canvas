Workflow

  1. Raise one hand and let the app scan it.
  2. That hand is locked for the session.
  3. Use hover to reach the toolbar, draw anywhere on the full screen.
  4. Use dedicated gestures for pen, eraser, colour, and clear.

Interaction Model

Actions are intentionally delayed slightly before activation, then placed on cooldown, which reduces false triggers and makes the app feel more controlled.

Action dwell Short hold before switching
Cooldown 2 seconds after each gesture action
